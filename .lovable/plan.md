

## Levoria Company Website — Implementation Plan

### Overview
A modern, minimalist single-page website for Levoria featuring a premium navy and gold design, smooth scrolling navigation, and thoughtfully structured content sections.

---

### Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Deep navy background (`#0a1628` / similar dark navy tones)
- Warm gold accents (inspired by logo, `#d4a853` / `#c9a050`)
- Off-white/cream text for contrast
- Subtle gray for secondary text

**Typography:**
- Inter font family throughout
- Clear size hierarchy (hero → section titles → body)
- Generous line-height for readability

**Components:**
- Rounded cards (14px radius) with very subtle borders
- Soft shadows for depth
- Smooth hover transitions throughout

---

### Site Structure

#### Header (Sticky Navigation)
- Levoria logo on the left (using your uploaded image)
- Navigation links: Home, Solutions, Team, Careers, Contact
- Animated underline on hover (left-to-right smooth transition)
- Compact, non-intrusive design
- Transparent background transitioning to solid on scroll

#### Section 1: Home (Hero)
- Bold headline: "Make complex systems understandable."
- Supportive subheadline about building human-friendly technology
- Two call-to-action buttons: "Explore Solutions" and "Contact Us"
- Three value pillar cards below:
  - **Clarity** — understand what's happening and why
  - **Reliability** — reduce uncertainty and risk
  - **Momentum** — move forward with confidence

#### Section 2: Solutions
- Brief introduction paragraph about Levoria's multi-solution approach
- **FeedBag.ai Feature Block:**
  - Two-column layout (text left, visuals right on desktop; stacked on mobile)
  - Full product description as provided
  - "Explore product" button (placeholder link)
  - Clean placeholder mockups for product imagery
  - Subtle "More solutions coming soon" note

#### Section 3: Team
- Short founder introduction paragraph
- Two founder cards side-by-side:
  - Placeholder photo
  - Name and role
  - One-line description
  - LinkedIn icon link

#### Section 4: Careers
- Title: "Careers & working style"
- Paragraph about how Levoria works
- Values list with subtle styling:
  - Ownership, Calm execution, High standards, Customer empathy, Learning mindset
- Clear note: "We're not hiring right now, but we're always happy to meet great people."

#### Section 5: Contact
- Clean contact form (visual mockup):
  - Name, Email, Message fields
  - Submit button with gold accent
- Contact email placeholder
- Social links (LinkedIn, X icons)

#### Footer
- Navigation links repeated
- Company/imprint placeholder
- Copyright notice
- Same navy background, subtle styling

---

### Interactive Details

- **Smooth scrolling** between all sections when clicking navigation
- **Navigation underline animation:** smooth left-to-right on hover
- **Button hover effects:** subtle color shifts and gentle scale
- **Cards:** light lift effect on hover
- **Section transitions:** subtle fade-in as sections enter viewport (optional polish)

---

### Responsive Design

- Mobile-first approach
- Full-width sections with max-width content container (~1200px)
- Stack layouts vertically on mobile (particularly the Solutions two-column)
- Hamburger menu on mobile with smooth open/close animation
- Touch-friendly tap targets
- Generous padding and spacing throughout

---

### Accessibility

- Strong color contrast (gold on navy, white text)
- Semantic HTML structure
- Keyboard navigation support
- Focus states on interactive elements
- Proper heading hierarchy
